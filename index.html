So What Is Mixing?

Mixing is the process of optimizing and blending recorded audio tracks into a final product, in this case your song. This is done through the use of many techniques. On this site I will talk you through the big three, which are balancing (adjusting relative volumes), equalizing (adjusting frequencies) and compression (reducing the loudest points of a sound). Use of these techniques works differently for different genres but the theory remains largely the same across all. Mixing is generally done at quieter volumes before being sent to a mastering engineer to be prepared for distribution.
