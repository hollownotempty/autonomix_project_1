What Is EQ?

Ask any music producer what tool they use most, and nine-out-of-ten times they’re going to say EQ (short for equaliser). In simple terms, EQ is a tool that allows you to turn down the level of individual sections of frequencies (or ‘frequency bands’) in a signal. It is used to pull out harsh frequencies or push up ones we like, allowing us to change the sonic character of a sound graphically.
